Button Mapping:

Down:	FFA857
Left:	FF22DD
Right:	FFC23D
OK:		FF02FD
1:		FF6897
2:		FF9867
3:		FFB04F

9:		FF5AA5
0:		FF4AB5
*:		FF42BD
#:		FF52AD


Wiring:
IR receiver Signal pin to D11
7-segment Data pin to A4
7-segment Clock pin to A5
Relay 1 trigger to D9
Relay 2 trigger to D8

Use 5v rail for all peripherals
